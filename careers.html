No longer you have to exhaust yourself by working 12 to 18 hours a day for 365 days. Life is more beautiful when we get to spend time on things we love doing all the way from gym, meditation, copywriting or any passionate projects.

WORK ON PROJECTS YOU LOVE

We believe it is real important to work on projects you love. The reason why is when you love a project and you believe in it, it activates your brain and you start taking inititaive and give inputs in building the best product possible. We believe in working with clients as a team.

NO MORE TRAVELLING FOR HOURS

Research proves one of the many reasons why developers lack productivity iin India s because of the hours they put in to travel from home to office in crowded public transport, traffic, and most importantly polluted environment. With us you no longer have to travel to work. You can work out of the nearest co-working space, starbucks or even from home. Whichever place boost your productivity, we are with you.

YOU ARE EMPOWERED

We do not like waiting, and we surely do not want our clients to wait. We are in the business of providing experience first. You as a representative of our company is empowered to make decisions to fulfil client expectations and provide them with the best experience.

ONE PROJECT AT A TIME

You only work on one project at a time. There are two reasons to this. First, we want you to be focused. Second, we want you to provide the best work possiblle. We do not enforce nor we allow you to work on multiple projects.

WE WORK AS A STARTUP

There is no corporate hierarchy, nor there will ever be. Whether we turn into a $100 million company or $100 billion. We will always work as a startup. No formal clothing at all. Wear what you are comfortable in. We do not want you wasting wearing formals and being uncomfortable. Just be you.

SPEND TIME WITH LOVED ONES

We feel, this is the most important of them all. We want you to have a social life, meet people, make connections, and spend time with your loved ones. Life is about human interactions.
